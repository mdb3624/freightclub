# FIN-401 — Automated Detention Pay Calculation & Billing

**As a** Trucker (Owner-Operator),
**I want** the platform to automatically calculate and record detention pay when I am held at a facility beyond the agreed free time,
**so that** I am fairly compensated for lost hours without needing to manually dispute or invoice the shipper.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] When a `DwellExceededEvent` is received from `OPS-402` (dwell > 2 hours), `DetentionCalculationService` computes: `detentionFee = (dwellMinutes − 120) / 60 × hourlyDetentionRate`; the `hourlyDetentionRate` is stored per tenant in `tenant_billing_config` (default $75/hr) — satisfies **REQ-401**
- [ ] `DetentionCharge` is a domain Value Object with `amount NUMERIC(10,2)`, `currencyCode VARCHAR(3)`, `loadId`, `tenantId`, and `calculatedAt TIMESTAMPTZ`; negative values are rejected by the constructor invariant
- [ ] `detention_charges` table includes `tenant_id` with RLS policy; `NUMERIC(10,4)` precision for all monetary columns; no `FLOAT` or `DOUBLE` — satisfies **REQ-103** and avoids floating-point drift
- [ ] Detention charge is recorded in `audit_log` with `event_type = 'DETENTION_CHARGED'` and full payload within the same `@Transactional` boundary as the calculation — satisfies **REQ-402**
- [ ] Shipper is notified via in-app notification: "Detention charge of $X applied to load #Y"; notification is dispatched via `message_outbox` relay (INF-202)
- [ ] Detention charges are included in the `FactoringOffer` net calculation in `FIN-502`; `DetentionCalculationService` exposes `getTotalDetentionForLoad(tenantId, loadId)` as an application-layer query
- [ ] `DetentionCalculationService` cyclomatic complexity ≤ 10 — satisfies **NFR-501**
- [ ] Composite index on `detention_charges(tenant_id, load_id)` for sub-second lookups — satisfies **NFR-503**

## Related Stories
- OPS-402 (dwell time tracking produces the DwellExceededEvent consumed here)
- FIN-502 (detention charges fold into factoring settlement)
- AUD-601 (detention charge must be in the immutable audit trail)
- INF-202 (shipper notification dispatched via outbox relay)
