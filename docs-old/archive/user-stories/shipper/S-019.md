# S-019 — Block Carriers

**As a** shipper,
**I want** to block specific carriers,
**so that** they cannot claim my future loads.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A "Block carrier" action is available on any load detail page where a trucker is or was assigned.
- [ ] Blocked carriers cannot claim or bid on any load posted by that shipper's tenant.
- [ ] If a blocked carrier attempts to claim a load, they receive no error (to avoid revealing the block); the claim silently fails from their perspective, and the load remains open.
- [ ] The shipper can view and manage their blocked carrier list from settings.
- [ ] The shipper can unblock a carrier at any time.
- [ ] Blocking is scoped to the shipper's tenant.
