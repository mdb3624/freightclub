# T-007 — HOS 70-Hour/8-Day Cycle Tracking

**As a** trucker,
**I want** the HOS widget to track my 70-hour/8-day cumulative on-duty cycle,
**so that** I remain FMCSA-compliant and don't unknowingly drive illegal hours.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The HOS widget includes a third tracking element: "70-hr / 8-day cycle" in addition to the existing 11-hr drive and 14-hr on-duty bars.
- [ ] The cycle counter shows total on-duty hours logged across the rolling 8-day window (today + prior 7 days).
- [ ] Hours remaining in the cycle (70 minus cumulative hours) are displayed prominently.
- [ ] A progress bar visually represents consumption of the 70-hour budget.
- [ ] The bar turns amber when ≤ 14 hours remain and red when ≤ 2 hours remain.
- [ ] The trucker can view a per-day breakdown of hours logged over the past 8 days.
- [ ] After a qualifying 34-hour restart, the cycle resets to 70 hours; the widget reflects this.
- [ ] The 70-hour counter is independent of the per-shift 11-hr and 14-hr limits.
- [ ] Data persists across sessions (hours logged are stored, not in-memory only).
