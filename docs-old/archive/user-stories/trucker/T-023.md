# T-023 — Rate Shipper After Delivery

**As a** trucker,
**I want** to rate a shipper after delivery,
**so that** the community has reliable data on which shippers pay on time and are easy to work with.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] After a load reaches DELIVERED status, the trucker is prompted to rate the shipper.
- [ ] Rating is 1–5 stars (required).
- [ ] Optional free-text review field (up to 500 characters).
- [ ] Optionally, the trucker can rate payment speed: Fast / On Time / Slow / Not Yet Paid.
- [ ] A rating can only be submitted once per load.
- [ ] The rating and review appear on the shipper's public profile (see S-027).
- [ ] The shipper is notified when a new review is received.
- [ ] Ratings cannot be edited by the trucker after 24 hours.
