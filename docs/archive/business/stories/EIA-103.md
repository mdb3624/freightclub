# EIA-103 — Diesel Price Week-over-Week Delta Indicator

**As a** Driver / Owner-Operator,
**I want** to see how diesel prices changed compared to the prior week for each region,
**so that** I can tell whether fuel costs are rising or falling and plan my load decisions accordingly.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] For each region, the ticker displays a week-over-week delta derived from the two most recent EIA weekly observations (e.g. `+$0.08` or `-$0.04`); unchanged displays `—` — satisfies **FR-05**
- [ ] A directional indicator accompanies each delta: ▲ for rising, ▼ for falling, — for unchanged — satisfies **FR-05**
- [ ] Rising prices render in red; falling prices render in green; consistent with FreightClub design system color tokens in both light and dark mode — satisfies **FR-08**
- [ ] Hovering or tapping a diesel price in the ticker reveals a tooltip showing the EIA reporting period (e.g. "Week of Mar 17") — satisfies **FR-04**
- [ ] **Security:** Delta computation is performed server-side within `EiaResponseParser`; the raw prior-week observation value is not included in the `/api/v1/market/fuel-prices` response payload, preventing reconstruction of full EIA response history — satisfies **NFR-11**
- [ ] Cyclomatic complexity ≤ 10 in `EiaResponseParser.computeDelta()` — satisfies reviewer checklist quality gate
- [ ] JaCoCo branch coverage ≥ 80% including: price increased; price decreased; price unchanged; prior-week observation missing (graceful null handling)

## Traceability
