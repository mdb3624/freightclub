# S-025 — In-Platform Payment to Carrier

**As a** shipper,
**I want** to pay carriers in-platform,
**so that** payments are traceable and disputes are easier to resolve.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Shipper can initiate payment from the load detail page once the load is DELIVERED.
- [ ] Payment triggers a transfer to the trucker's bank account on file.
- [ ] The shipper sees a payment confirmation screen with amount, recipient, and expected arrival date.
- [ ] Both shipper and trucker receive notifications when payment is initiated and when it clears.
- [ ] Payments are recorded in a payment history accessible from the load detail and from a global payments page.
- [ ] A payment can only be initiated once per load (idempotent).
- [ ] Failed payments surface a clear error with retry option.
