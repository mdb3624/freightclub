# S-026 — Rate and Review Trucker

**As a** shipper,
**I want** to rate and review a trucker after delivery,
**so that** the platform builds reliable reputation data that helps other shippers make informed decisions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] After a load reaches DELIVERED status, the shipper is prompted (via in-app notification) to rate the trucker.
- [ ] Rating is 1–5 stars (required).
- [ ] An optional free-text review field (up to 500 characters) is available.
- [ ] A rating can only be submitted once per load.
- [ ] The submitted rating and review appear on the trucker's public profile.
- [ ] The trucker is notified when a new review is posted (in-app).
- [ ] Ratings older than 24 hours cannot be edited or deleted by the shipper.
