# S-031 — Cancel Load with Reason

**As a** shipper,
**I want** to cancel a load with a required reason field,
**so that** the assigned trucker understands why the load was cancelled.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The cancel confirmation dialog (see S-006) includes a required "Reason for cancellation" text area.
- [ ] The confirm button is disabled until a reason of at least 10 characters is entered.
- [ ] The cancellation reason is stored on the load record (or a LoadEvent with `event_type = CANCELLED` includes the reason).
- [ ] The reason is visible to the trucker on their load history detail page.
- [ ] The reason is visible to the shipper on their load detail page.
- [ ] Reason is plain text; minimum 10 characters, maximum 500 characters.
- [ ] When a load is cancelled with a reason, a `LoadEvent` row is created with `event_type = CANCELLED` and the reason stored.
