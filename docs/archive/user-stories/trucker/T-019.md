# T-019 — HOS Proactive Warnings at 4-Hour and 2-Hour Thresholds

**As a** trucker,
**I want** proactive HOS warnings at 4-hour and 2-hour remaining thresholds,
**so that** I have enough lead time to plan a stop before I'm legally required to pull over.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] When 4 hours remain on either the 11-hr drive limit or 14-hr on-duty window, the relevant bar turns amber and displays a warning: "4 hours remaining — plan your next stop."
- [ ] When 2 hours remain, the bar turns red and the warning escalates: "2 hours remaining — stop soon."
- [ ] When 30 minutes remain, an additional urgent warning is shown: "30 minutes remaining — find a safe stop immediately."
- [ ] Warnings apply independently to each limit (drive, on-duty, 70-hr cycle).
- [ ] Thresholds apply to the 70-hr/8-day cycle bar as well (at 14 hr and 2 hr remaining).
- [ ] Warning states are visually distinct from each other — amber ≠ red ≠ gray.
- [ ] When the trucker is not in an active shift, no warnings are shown.
