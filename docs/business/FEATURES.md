# FreightClub Platform Features

FreightClub is a load board connecting **Shippers** (freight senders) and **Owner-Operators** (independent truckers) to post, find, and complete freight loads. It simplifies the logistics workflow while providing visibility, fair pricing, and trust through ratings and reviews.

---

## Shipper Features

What shippers can do on FreightClub to manage their freight.

### Post a Load

Post your freight for delivery. Enter pickup location, delivery location, dates, weight, and what rate you're offering. Your load goes live on the board immediately or you can save it as a draft to publish later.

**User benefit:** Get your freight picked up faster by reaching truckers across the network instead of juggling phone calls.

### Track Your Loads
See where each load is: Draft, Posted, Claimed by a trucker, Picked Up, Delivered, or Cancelled. View all your active loads in one dashboard with a summary of status counts.

**User benefit:** Know exactly what's in motion and what's waiting—no guessing where your shipments are.

### Manage Load Drafts
Save a load as a draft and publish it later. Edit the details (destination, rate, dates) before publishing. Only you can see drafts—they're not visible to truckers until you publish.

**User benefit:** Plan shipments ahead of time without committing them to the board.

### Generate a Bill of Lading
When you publish a load, FreightClub automatically generates a Bill of Lading (BOL)—the official shipping document. Download it, share it, print it.

**User benefit:** No manual paperwork for every load. A professional BOL is ready the moment your shipment goes live.

### Rate and Review Truckers
After a delivery is complete, rate the trucker 1–5 stars and leave a comment about the experience (on-time, careful, professional). Your feedback is visible on their public profile.

**User benefit:** Build trust in the network. Help other shippers know who's reliable. Hold truckers accountable.

### View Trucker Profiles
See a trucker's average rating, delivery history, equipment types they operate, and feedback from other shippers before you decide to work with them.

**User benefit:** Make informed decisions about who you trust with your freight.

### Cancel a Load
If you need to pull a posted load off the board, cancel it with a reason message (e.g., "Customer postponed shipment"). The assigned trucker gets notified why you cancelled.

**User benefit:** Flexibility to respond to business changes without penalties.

### View Fuel Price Data
See regional diesel prices (East, Midwest, South, Rocky Mountain, West) and weekly price trends to benchmark your rates and understand market conditions.

**User benefit:** Price your loads competitively. Know when fuel costs are rising so you can adjust rates.

## Trucker (Owner-Operator) Features

What truckers can do to find and manage loads.

### Browse the Load Board
Search and filter open loads from all shippers. Filter by pickup state, delivery state, equipment needed (Box, Flatbed, Tanker, Reefer, Auto Carrier, Specialized), and date ranges. See the rate per mile, distance, and estimated profit.

**User benefit:** Find loads that match your equipment and route. Spend less time searching.

### See Load Profitability
When you browse loads, FreightClub calculates profit potential based on your cost profile:
- **Green:** High profit (above your target)
- **Yellow:** Neutral profit (close to your target)
- **Red:** Low profit (below your target)

No more guessing if a load pays enough.

**User benefit:** Accept only loads that make financial sense for your business. Make data-driven decisions, not just desperate ones.

### Track Your Operating Costs
Enter your monthly expenses (truck payment, insurance, fuel efficiency, maintenance costs, per diem, permits) and your target profit margin. FreightClub calculates your minimum acceptable revenue per mile (RPM). This number feeds into load profitability scores so you see instantly if a load is worth your time.

**User benefit:** Know your break-even point. Stop hauling loads that lose money.

### Claim a Load
Find a load you want and claim it. You're now the assigned trucker for that shipment. The shipper gets notified. You're locked in until you pick up, deliver, or cancel.

**User benefit:** Reserve loads before someone else grabs them. Build a predictable schedule.

### Mark Load Milestones
- Mark the load as **Picked Up** when you collect the freight.
- Mark it as **Delivered** when you drop it off.

The shipper and FreightClub get notified at each step so everyone knows the shipment is moving.

**User benefit:** Real-time visibility for shippers. Peace of mind knowing your delivery is tracked.

### Upload Proof of Delivery
Take a photo of the Bill of Lading (BOL) when you pick up. Take a photo of the delivery signature or location when you drop off. Upload them directly in the app—no scanning, no emails, no lost documents.

**User benefit:** Digital proof that the shipment was delivered. Protects you in disputes.

### Report Issues with a Load
If something's wrong—damage, incorrect weight, hazmat violation, shipper wasn't ready—report it with photos and description. The shipper gets notified immediately.

**User benefit:** Document problems while they happen. Evidence for disputes or claims.

### Rate and Review Shippers
After delivery, rate the shipper 1–5 stars (on-time payment, good load info, professional). Your feedback is visible on their public profile.

**User benefit:** Hold shippers accountable. Help other truckers know who pays on time and who gives accurate load info.

### View Your Load History
See all loads you've completed: dates, routes, rates, and ratings you received from shippers.

**User benefit:** Portfolio of your work. Proof of experience for new brokers or customers.

### See Hours of Service (HOS) Tracking
Coming soon: Track your hours on duty, off duty, sleeper berth, and driving—federal compliance in the app.

**User benefit:** Stay compliant with FMCSA regulations. Avoid violations and out-of-service orders.

## Shared Platform Features

Features that benefit both shippers and truckers.

### User Registration & Login
Sign up as a Shipper (create company) or Trucker (join existing company or create new one). Register with email and password. Log in anytime to access your loads and matches.

**User benefit:** Secure, role-specific accounts. Your company data stays private.

### Multi-User Companies
Invite team members to join your company (shipper office staff, multiple drivers, dispatchers). Each gets their own login. You control access via company join codes.

**User benefit:** Collaboration without sharing passwords. Large teams can work together.

### In-App Notifications
Get notified instantly when:
- A trucker claims your load (shipper)
- A load is picked up or delivered (shipper)
- A load is available to claim (trucker)
- A shipper cancels a load (trucker)

Notifications appear in the app and via email. Mark them read, or clear all at once.

**User benefit:** Never miss important updates. Stay on top of your shipments in real time.

### Export Documents & History
Download and export loads as PDF (includes all metadata, photos, ratings, delivery proof, BOL).

**User benefit:** Archive shipment records. Share with accountants, auditors, or insurance.

### View Your Public Profile
Shippers and truckers each have a public profile showing:
- **Average rating** (1–5 stars)
- **Delivery/work history** (count of completed loads)
- **Feedback comments** from counterparties

Others see this before deciding to work with you.

**User benefit:** Build reputation. Trust is currency in trucking. Your track record speaks for itself.

## Advanced Features for Owner-Operators

### Calculate Cost Per Mile (CPM)
Input your monthly fixed costs (truck payment/lease, insurance, IFTA/IRP, phone/ELD, per diem) and variable costs (fuel price per gallon, MPG, maintenance cost per mile). FreightClub calculates your CPM and minimum acceptable profit margin in real time.

**Formula:**
```
Fixed CPM = Total Monthly Costs ÷ Monthly Miles
Fuel CPM = Diesel Price ÷ MPG
Maintenance CPM = Maintenance per Mile
Total CPM = Fixed CPM + Fuel CPM + Maintenance CPM
Minimum RPM = Total CPM + Your Target Profit
```

**User benefit:** Never guess if a load is profitable. Automate your break-even math with real numbers.

### See Real-Time Diesel Prices
View current regional diesel prices (5 U.S. regions) and weekly trends. Use this to adjust your CPM calculation and load bids.

**User benefit:** Stay ahead of fuel market swings. Price your minimum rates based on current conditions.

## Platform Standards

### Secure Authentication
All accounts use encrypted passwords and secure login tokens. Your payment info is never stored on our platform—only by secure third parties.

### Multi-Tenant Isolation
Each company's data is completely isolated. No shipper can see another shipper's loads. No trucker can see another trucker's rates.

### Fair Pricing
Load board rates are market-driven by shipper posting and trucker demand. FreightClub charges a small 2% commission on successful deliveries—transparent and competitive.

### Soft Deletes
Nothing is permanently deleted. Cancelled loads, removed files, and closed accounts are archived forever for audit and compliance.
