# US-201: Email Notifications (Claim/Pickup/Delivery/Cancel)

**Phase:** 2 — Notifications & EIA Integration  
**Status:** COMPLETED  
**Depends On:** US-103  
**Blocks:** US-202, all Phase 3+ features

---

## User Story

As a **shipper or trucker**, I need to receive email notifications when loads are claimed, picked up, delivered, or cancelled so that I stay informed about my freight in real-time without checking the app constantly.

## Business Rules

- Notifications are triggered by domain events (ClaimEvent, PickupEvent, DeliveryEvent, CancelEvent).
- Emails are queued asynchronously; the API request returns immediately.
- Email service integrates with SendGrid or AWS SES for delivery.
- Notification preferences are configurable per tenant (opt-in/opt-out by event type).
- Shipper email: company admin email from `ShipperProfile`.
- Trucker email: user email from registration.
- Email delivery is retried 3 times on transient failure (429, 5xx).

---

## Implementation Notes

- **Backend:** `EmailService` integrates with external email provider; `NotificationService` publishes events; `NotificationController` exposes preferences API.
- **Event Publishing:** `ClaimEvent`, `PickupEvent`, `DeliveryEvent`, `CancelEvent` published from domain layer.
- **Async Queue:** Events consumed by `NotificationListener` which calls `EmailService.send()`.
- **Caching:** Notification preferences cached with 1-minute TTL (NFR-504).
- **Tests:** `NotificationServiceTest.java` validates event-to-email mapping; `EmailServiceTest.java` mocks external provider.
