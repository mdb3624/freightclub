# US-302-v2: BOL Pickup Attestation (Carrier Confirm+Lock)

**Phase:** 3 — Document Management
**Status:** IN_PROGRESS
**Depends On:** US-302, US-303
**Origin:** CHG-855 (`/roast` council review of the BOL workflow, 2026-07-14)

---

## User Story

As an **owner-operator/carrier**, I need to confirm pickup with a timestamped attestation (and optionally note exceptions like damage or shortage) so that the platform-generated BOL becomes a legally meaningful, locked record instead of a document neither party ever attested to.

---

## Background

CHG-855 documented a five-persona `/roast` review of the existing US-302 BOL workflow. The council converged: a BOL's legal/evidentiary value comes from the carrier's attestation of condition/count/time at physical pickup, not from the shipper's pre-populated data alone. This matches how existing industry platforms (Uber Freight, C.H. Robinson eBOL, the NMFTA Digital LTL Council standard) and FMCSA carrier-liability transfer actually work: shipper drafts, carrier confirms/signs at pickup.

Today (US-302, COMPLETED), the BOL auto-generates from shipper data with no carrier attestation step — this story adds that step without reworking the completed shipper-side generation.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

| ID | Criterion |
|---|---|
| AC1 | OO can confirm pickup with an optional exception note (free text) and optional exception photo. |
| AC2 | Confirming pickup creates an immutable attestation record (trucker id, timestamp, notes) tied to the load. |
| AC3 | The platform-generated BOL document is marked `locked` at the moment of attestation and cannot be regenerated/altered afterward. |
| AC4 | Shipper-side document list shows a "Locked" indicator on the BOL once attested. |
| AC5 | Existing BOL-photo-required gate on `markPickedUp` is preserved unchanged (this feature is additive, not a replacement). |

---

## Business Rules

- Attestation is additive to, not a replacement of, the existing BOL-photo-upload gate on `markPickedUp`.
- One attestation per load (`load_id` UNIQUE on `bol_attestations`).
- Exception notes/photo are optional — a clean pickup requires only the timestamp + trucker id.
- Locking is permanent: once `load_documents.locked = true`, no further edits to that document are permitted by this story (regeneration/edit flows are out of scope — none currently exist for `BOL_GENERATED`).
