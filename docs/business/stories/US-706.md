## User Story

**As a** *Shipper or Admin*,  
**I want to** *view revenue, commission costs, and profitability by load, lane, and carrier*,  
**so that** *I can identify high-margin lanes, optimize pricing, and forecast profitability.*

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-1: Shipper Views Revenue Dashboard

**Given** a shipper is logged in  
**When** they navigate to Finance > Revenue  
**Then** display:
- Total revenue (all-time, last 30/90 days)
- Total loads in period
- Average revenue per load
- Commission paid (2% of revenue)
- Net revenue (revenue - commission)

**Example:**
```
Revenue Dashboard (Last 30 Days)
────────────────────────────────
Total Revenue:       $127,450
Commission (2%):     $(2,549)
Net Revenue:         $124,901

Loads:               847
Avg Revenue/Load:    $150.41
```

### AC-4: Profitability Forecast

**Given** a shipper wants to forecast quarterly revenue  
**When** they click "Forecast > Revenue"  
**Then** display:
- Projected revenue (7/30/90 day forecast)
- Based on historical rate × forecasted load volume
- Confidence interval (80%, 95%)
- Seasonality adjustment

**Example:**
```
Revenue Forecast (Next 30 Days)
───────────────────────────────
Historical Avg:      $127,450/month
Forecasted:          $135,200/month (+6%)
Confidence:          85% (80-90% range)

Seasonality:         +4% (spring peak season)
Load Forecast:       890 loads (vs. 847 actual)
```

### AC-7: Multi-Tenancy & Data Isolation

**Given** any revenue query  
**When** shipper/admin requests financial data  
**Then** MUST:
- Show only loads and revenue for their tenant
- Show only carriers within their tenant
- Commission calculation specific to their settlement terms
- RLS policy: `SELECT WHERE tenant_id = app.current_tenant_id`

## Technical Context

### Domain Entities
```
LoadFinancial (Immutable, Audit-Trail)
- loadId, tenantId, shipperId, carrierId
- postedAt, claimedAt, completedAt
- ratePerMile (decimal), totalRevenue (decimal)
- commission (2% hardcoded), netRevenue (revenue - commission)

LaneRevenue (Pre-Computed, Daily Refresh)
- origin, destination, tenantId, date (daily bucket)
- loadCount, totalRevenue, totalCommission, netRevenue
- avgRate, margin%, trend

CarrierRevenue (Pre-Computed, Daily Refresh)
- carrierId, tenantId, date (daily bucket)
- loadsAssigned, totalRevenue, avgRate, netRevenue, margin%
```

### Database Changes
- New `load_financial` table (immutable, append-only)
- New `lane_revenue_daily` table (pre-computed, refreshed daily)
- New `carrier_revenue_daily` table (pre-computed, refreshed daily)
- Indexes: (tenant_id, date), (shipper_id), (carrier_id)
- RLS policies on all tables

### Algorithm
```
When settlement completes:
  INSERT into load_financial(loadId, tenantId, totalRevenue, commission, netRevenue)
  TRIGGER: refresh_lane_revenue_daily() -- aggregate by lane
  TRIGGER: refresh_carrier_revenue_daily() -- aggregate by carrier
  TRIGGER: compute_margin_analysis() -- margin by equipment type

Background Job (Daily):
  RECALCULATE all *_daily tables FROM load_financial
  RECALCULATE forecast_revenue_30day FROM historical trend
  RECALCULATE seasonality_adjustment FROM historical data
```
