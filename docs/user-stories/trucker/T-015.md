# T-015 — Toast Confirmation After Claim

**As a** trucker,
**I want** a toast confirmation immediately after claiming a load,
**so that** I know the claim registered successfully and I'm not left wondering if my click worked.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] After a successful load claim, a toast notification appears within 1 second reading "Load claimed! Check your active load for details." (or equivalent).
- [ ] The toast includes a link/button to navigate directly to the active load view.
- [ ] The toast auto-dismisses after 5 seconds or can be manually dismissed.
- [ ] If the claim fails (e.g., load already taken by another trucker), an error toast appears: "This load was just claimed by another driver." — the load board refreshes to reflect the updated status.
- [ ] No success toast appears on a failed claim.
