# T-016 — Clear Explanation When Claim Is Blocked

**As a** trucker,
**I want** a clear explanation when I try to claim a load but already have an active load,
**so that** I understand why the board appears locked and what I need to do.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] When a trucker has an active load (CLAIMED or IN_TRANSIT), the "Claim" button on all other loads is disabled.
- [ ] Hovering over or clicking a disabled Claim button shows a tooltip or inline message: "You already have an active load. Complete or wait for your current load to claim a new one."
- [ ] The load board does not go silently blank — loads remain visible even when claiming is disabled.
- [ ] A banner or prominent notice at the top of the load board states the trucker has an active load, with a link to view it.
- [ ] Once the active load reaches DELIVERED or CANCELLED, the Claim buttons re-enable automatically on next data refresh.
